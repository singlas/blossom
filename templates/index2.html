Why is this important?

Relationship research can predict to 90% accuracy whether a relationship will end in divorce by listening to a couple talk for 5 minutes about an issue between them. This is even before they get married. There are important patterns that if identified and honed can help you create a lasting loving relationship.

Get Your Report
How does it work?

You record you and your partner talking to each other for upto 5 hours in any given week. Submit your audio file to us. Our relationship experts consisting of therapists, counsellors and coaches analyze and send you data on your relationship heath. You can repeat this in following weeks with a new report in order to gather trend and track your relationship health improving.

Get Your Report
What kind of data will I receive?

You will recieve data on your communication patterns such as your positive expression to negative expression ratio. You will also receive examples of when you and your partner exemplified both positive and negative forms of communication. This data will help you identify where your strenghts are and where you could improve. Bringing more lasting joy to your relationship.
